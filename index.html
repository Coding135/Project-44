KINDNESS


A Game By: Parth Singh


Music By:



RULES:


  1. Use Arrow Keys To Move The Kind Person (You)
  2. Use The Space Bar To Jump Over The Hurdles

STORY INFO:


  1. There Will Be Hurdles That Will Appear Every Few Seconds Continously
  2. The Height Of The Hurdles Will Increase As You Progress Through The Game
  3. There Will Be An Evil Person Who Will Drop A Fireball On The Hurdle. Every Time The Fireball Touches The Hurdle, The Height Of The Hurdle Will Increase
  4. The Evil Person Will Be Flying In The Sky
